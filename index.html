My Projects

Tenant To Scale - Another GMTK Game Jam game I have made. This one was for 2024, and I felt much more comfortable making this one. I learned a lot about using animations in unity and how to balance the difficulty while still providing a challenge. It was very rewarding to see people play this one.

God Vs Gravity - This was my submission to the GMTK 2023 Game Jam. I spent some time after the Jam to add some polish. This was my first time in a Game Jam and I really appreciate the experience I got from making this. I plan on making a game for the next GMTK Game Jam.

Justins Peanut Factory - I am a big fan of games where the goal is just to make numbers go up. It's such a simple concept yet it can make such a fun gameplay loop! This was my first attempt at making a cookie clicker game.

Bouncing Gage - This was a quick project after a couple of other prototypes that I did not finish. I think it's very important to finish projects before trying other things and I wanted to make sure I could still make something simple with a little polish and mark as complete.

Paper Wars - This was my first attempt at making a game without following a tutorial. This might have been the project that taught me the most about making games and just how much fun it could be!

Sticky Walls - My second attempt at making a game. I watched a tutorial for a large portion of development but I started adding my own elements and changing things from the tutorial. Overall I am very happy with how this one turned out.

Cube Game the Game - The first game I ever made. I am sure many people started with the same game before as it comes from a very popular tutorial, I learned so much starting here and I am so glad that I have this to show from it.
